<p><ul><li>Resonant Membrane</li><li>Periodic Resonators</li><li>Sonic Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic metamaterial market consists of three main types: Resonant Membrane, Periodic Resonators, and Sonic Crystal. Resonant Membrane structures consist of vibrating membranes that can manipulate sound frequencies. Periodic Resonators use small resonant structures to control sound waves. Sonic Crystal structures utilize periodic arrays of scatterers to control sound propagation. Each type of acoustic metamaterial offers unique capabilities in manipulating sound waves, making them suitable for various applications such as noise control, acoustic cloaking, and sound filtering.</p></p>
