<p><p>Needle-free drug delivery systems are advanced technologies that allow drugs to be delivered into the body without the use of traditional needles. There are three main types of needle-free drug delivery systems: jet-based, spring-based, and laser-based. Jet-based systems use a high-pressure stream of liquid to deliver drugs, spring-based systems utilize a spring mechanism to push drugs into the skin, and laser-based systems use laser technology to create tiny channels in the skin for drug delivery. These innovative systems offer a more convenient and pain-free alternative to traditional needle injections.</p></p>
