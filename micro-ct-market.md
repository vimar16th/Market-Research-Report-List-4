<p><p>Micro-CT is used in various markets, including structural imaging and functional imaging. Structural imaging involves high-resolution scans to study the internal structure of materials, tissues, and small organisms. This is useful in research, quality control, and medical diagnostics. On the other hand, functional imaging focuses on dynamic processes within the sample, providing valuable insights into physiological functions and changes over time. Both markets offer different applications and benefits for industries such as healthcare, material science, and life sciences.</p></p>
