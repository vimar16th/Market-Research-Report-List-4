<p><p>Single Orifice Air Valves are available in two main market types: Flanged and Threaded. Flanged air valves are designed with flanges that allow for direct connection to pipelines, ensuring a secure and leak-proof fit. On the other hand, Threaded air valves feature threads that facilitate easy installation onto pipes without the need for additional fittings. Both types offer efficient air release and vacuum protection in pipelines, catering to different installation requirements in various industries.</p></p>
