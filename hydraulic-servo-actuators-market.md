<p><p>Hydraulic servo actuators are classified into two main types: Single-Ended and Double-Ended servo hydraulic actuators. Single-Ended actuators use a piston on one side of the cylinder to generate linear motion, while Double-Ended actuators have pistons on both sides. Single-Ended actuators are simpler and more cost-effective, while Double-Ended actuators provide more balanced force and motion control. Both types are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and industrial manufacturing for precise and powerful motion control applications.</p></p>
