<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Exhaust Fans are categorized into two main types based on their design and functionality. Centrifugal Fans are designed to move air radially outwards and are ideal for applications requiring high pressure and airflow, such as ventilation systems. On the other hand, Axial Fans are designed to move air parallel to the shaft axis and are suitable for applications requiring high airflow at low pressure, such as cooling applications. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining indoor air quality and temperature control in commercial establishments.</p></p>
