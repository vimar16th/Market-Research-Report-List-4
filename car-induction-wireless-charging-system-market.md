<p><p>The car induction wireless charging system market includes two main types: electromagnetic induction and magnetic resonance. Electromagnetic induction technology uses a magnetic field to transfer energy from a transmitter to a receiver in the vehicle. On the other hand, magnetic resonance technology involves the creation of a magnetic field between two coils that are tuned to the same frequency, allowing for efficient wireless charging of electric vehicles. Both technologies offer convenient and efficient ways to charge electric vehicles without the need for physical connections.</p></p>
