<p><p>Tower cranes rental service market can be categorized into two main types: mobile type and fixed type. Mobile type tower cranes are mounted on wheels or tracks, allowing for easy mobility around construction sites. These cranes are ideal for projects that require frequent repositioning. On the other hand, fixed type tower cranes are anchored to the ground and offer greater stability and lifting capacity. They are suitable for projects that require heavy lifting operations over an extended period of time.</p></p>
