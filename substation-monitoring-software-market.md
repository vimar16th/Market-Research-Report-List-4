<p><p>Substation monitoring software is utilized in three main market types: asset management, production management, and performance management. Asset management focuses on tracking and maintaining equipment within the substation to optimize its lifespan and efficiency. Production management involves monitoring the overall production output of the substation to ensure consistent and reliable energy supply. Performance management assesses the performance of the substation in terms of efficiency, reliability, and safety. Each market type plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation of the substation.</p></p>
