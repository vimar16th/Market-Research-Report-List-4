<p><p>3D Printing in Healthcare market includes various types such as Extrusion, Photo-polymerization, Laser Beam Melting, and Others. Extrusion involves depositing material layer by layer to create a 3D object. Photo-polymerization uses light to solidify liquid resin into a solid object. Laser Beam Melting uses a laser to melt and fuse metal powder into a solid. Other types may include powder bed fusion, binder jetting, and electron beam melting, each offering unique advantages for healthcare applications.</p></p>
