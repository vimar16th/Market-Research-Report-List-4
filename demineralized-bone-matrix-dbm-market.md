<p><p>Demineralized Bone Matrix (DBM) is a type of bone graft material commonly used in orthopedic surgeries to promote bone healing and regeneration. DBM is available in various forms including Gel, Putty, Putty with Chips, and others. Gel DBM is a semi-solid form used for filling voids and gaps in bone tissue. Putty DBM is moldable and can be easily shaped to fit different bone defects. Putty with Chips DBM contains small bone chips for added structural support. Other forms of DBM may include paste or powder formulations.</p></p>
