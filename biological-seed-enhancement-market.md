<p><p>Biological seed enhancement market includes biofertilizers and biostimulants. Biofertilizers are natural fertilizers that contain beneficial microorganisms which enhance nutrient availability in the soil. They promote plant growth and increase crop yield. Biostimulants, on the other hand, are substances that promote plant growth and development independently of their nutrient content. They enhance plant tolerance to stress and improve overall crop quality. Both biofertilizers and biostimulants play a crucial role in sustainable agriculture by improving soil health and plant resilience.</p></p>
