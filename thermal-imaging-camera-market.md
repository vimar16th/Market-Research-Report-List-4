<p><p>Thermal imaging cameras are classified into two main types based on their technology - uncooled thermal imaging cameras and cooled thermal imaging cameras. Uncooled thermal imaging cameras are more cost-effective and require less maintenance, making them popular for consumer applications like surveillance and automotive night vision. Cooled thermal imaging cameras, on the other hand, offer higher sensitivity and image quality, making them suitable for military, industrial, and scientific applications where accuracy and precision are crucial. Both types play a key role in various industries for temperature detection and monitoring.</p></p>
