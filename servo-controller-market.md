<p><p>A Single-Axis Motion Controller is designed to control the motion of a single servo motor in industrial automation applications. It provides precise positioning and velocity control for one axis of movement. On the other hand, a Multi-Axis Motion Controller is capable of controlling multiple servo motors simultaneously, allowing for synchronized movement in complex systems. These controllers are essential in robotics, CNC machines, and other automated systems that require coordinated motion across multiple axes.</p></p>
