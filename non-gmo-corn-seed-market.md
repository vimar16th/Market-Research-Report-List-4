<p><p>Non-GMO corn seed market types include double hybrid, single cross hybrid, and triple cross hybrid. Double hybrid corn seeds are created by crossing two inbred lines, resulting in high-yielding and genetically diverse plants. Single cross hybrid seeds involve the crossing of two genetically different inbred lines to produce vigorous plants with uniform characteristics. Triple cross hybrid seeds are created by crossing three different inbred lines, combining the best traits of each parent for superior yield and quality. These market types cater to farmers looking for diverse options in non-GMO corn seeds.</p></p>
