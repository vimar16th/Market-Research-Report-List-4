MFGM is a bioactive membrane in milk containing proteins and lipids with nutritional benefits. Phospholipids are essential components of cell membranes and have emulsifying properties in food products. Gangliosides are a type of sphingolipid with potential health benefits for brain function and immunity. These fractions cater to different consumer demands for functional and nutritional ingredients in food and beverage products.</p></p>
