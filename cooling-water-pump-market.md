<p><p>Cooling water pumps are used in various industries for cooling systems. Dry cooling systems, on the other hand, use air as the cooling medium, with the pump circulating air through the system to cool it down. Both types of cooling water pumps are essential for maintaining proper temperature levels in industrial processes and equipment.</p></p>
