<p><p>The commercial compactor market can be divided into two main types: fixed and detached. Fixed compactors are permanently attached to a building or structure, typically used for high-volume waste management in commercial settings. Detached compactors, on the other hand, are standalone units that can be moved around and placed at various locations. They are commonly used in locations where space is limited or where waste needs to be compacted in different areas. Both types serve the same purpose of reducing waste volume and improving overall waste management efficiency.</p></p>
