<p><p>Suspended platforms come in two main market types: round platform and square platform. Round platforms provide a smooth and continuous surface for workers to move around on, offering equal weight distribution to ensure stability. On the other hand, square platforms offer a more spacious and versatile working area, ideal for larger projects that require more space for equipment and materials. Both types of platforms are commonly used in construction and maintenance projects where workers need to access hard-to-reach areas at height.</p></p>
