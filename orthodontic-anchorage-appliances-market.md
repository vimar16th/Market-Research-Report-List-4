<p><ul><li>Bands and Buccal Tubes</li><li>Miniscrews</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthodontic Anchorage Appliances like Bands and Buccal Tubes are traditional methods used to provide anchorage during orthodontic treatment. They are typically attached to the teeth using dental cement and provide support and stability for the movement of other teeth. On the other hand, Miniscrews are a newer form of anchorage appliance that are smaller in size and can be directly inserted into the jawbone to provide more precise control over tooth movement. They are often used in cases where traditional methods are not effective or feasible.</p></p>
