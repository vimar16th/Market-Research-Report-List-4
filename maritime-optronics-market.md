<p><p>The Maritime Optronics market includes Multispectral and Hyperspectral technologies for use in naval applications. Multispectral technology captures and processes images across multiple wavelengths to provide enhanced detection and identification capabilities. Hyperspectral technology goes a step further by capturing hundreds of narrow spectral bands to provide detailed information about a target's composition. Both technologies are crucial for enhancing situational awareness and decision-making in maritime environments, making them valuable assets for defense and security applications.</p></p>
