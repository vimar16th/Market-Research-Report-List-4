Rb gene therapy focuses on restoring the function of the retinoblastoma gene to prevent uncontrolled cell growth. P53 gene therapy aims to induce programmed cell death in cancer cells. APC gene therapy targets mutations in the adenomatous polyposis coli gene involved in colorectal cancer. NF1 gene therapy aims to correct defects in the neurofibromin gene associated with neurofibromatosis type 1. Other gene therapies target specific genetic mutations driving various types of cancer.</p></p>
