<p><p>The EEG-EMG equipment market consists of two main types: Electroencephalography (EEG) and Electromyography (EMG). EEG equipment is used to monitor and record the electrical activity of the brain, helping to diagnose conditions such as epilepsy and sleep disorders. On the other hand, EMG equipment is used to measure the electrical activity produced by skeletal muscles, aiding in the diagnosis of neuromuscular disorders. Both types of equipment play important roles in the field of neurology and are essential tools for healthcare providers.</p></p>
