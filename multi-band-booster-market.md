<p><p>Multi-band boosters are devices used to boost cellular signals across various frequency bands. Dual band boosters operate on two frequency bands, typically covering the most popular bands used by major carriers. Tri-band boosters cover three bands, offering increased coverage and carrier compatibility. Quad-band boosters operate on four bands, providing even greater coverage and flexibility. Five-band boosters cover five bands, ensuring compatibility with a wide range of carriers and frequencies. Each type of booster caters to different needs and preferences in signal boosting.</p></p>
