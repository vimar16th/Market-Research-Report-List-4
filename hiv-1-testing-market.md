<p><ul><li>Antibody</li><li>Nucleic Acid Testing</li><li>Antigen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HIV-1 testing market encompasses three main types of testing methods: antibody testing, nucleic acid testing, and antigen testing. Antibody testing detects the presence of antibodies produced by the body in response to HIV infection. Nucleic acid testing directly detects the genetic material of the virus, providing an early and accurate diagnosis. Antigen testing identifies specific proteins produced by the virus itself. Each type of testing plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring HIV-1 infection.</p></p>
