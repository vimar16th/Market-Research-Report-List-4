<p><p>Green Roofing Systems can be categorized into three main types: Intensive, Extensive, and Others. Intensive green roofs have a greater depth of soil and can accommodate a wider variety of plants, including trees and shrubs. Extensive green roofs have a shallower layer of soil and are typically planted with low-maintenance vegetation like grasses and sedums. Other types of green roofs include semi-intensive and biodiverse roofs, which fall somewhere in between intensive and extensive in terms of soil depth and plant diversity.</p></p>
