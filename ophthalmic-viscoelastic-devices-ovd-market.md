<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs) are used during surgical procedures to protect and maintain the shape of the eye. There are four main types of OVDs on the market: Dispersives OVDs, which have lower viscosity and spread easily; Cohesive OVDs, which have higher viscosity and stay in place; Combination OVDs, which combine properties of both Dispersives and Cohesives; and Visco-Adaptive OVDs, which change their viscosity based on the amount of stress applied. Each type offers unique benefits for different surgical needs.</p></p>
