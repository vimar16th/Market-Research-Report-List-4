<p><p>Food extrusion equipment market can be categorized into two main types: single-screw extruders and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders are known for their simplicity and are commonly used for producing products like pasta, snacks, and pet food. On the other hand, twin-screw extruders are more versatile and can handle a wider range of ingredients and processes, making them suitable for applications such as breakfast cereals, protein extrusion, and textured vegetable protein. Both types of extruders play a crucial role in the food industry for processing various food products efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
