<p><p>Track-etched membranes are categorized into three main types based on the material used: polycarbonate, polyester, and polyimide. Polycarbonate type membranes are known for their high mechanical strength and thermal resistance. Polyester type membranes are preferred for their chemical resistance and low protein binding properties. Polyimide type membranes are valued for their high temperature stability and compatibility with aggressive solvents. Each type offers unique properties suitable for various applications in filtration, separation, and microscopy.</p></p>
