<p><p>Absolute pressure sensors are used in various industries for measuring pressure accurately. They can be classified into different types based on their working principle. Piezoresistive sensors use a change in resistance to measure pressure, capacitive sensors use changes in capacitance, resonant sensors use frequency changes, electromagnetic sensors use magnetic fields, optical sensors use light properties, and others like potentiometric, piezoelectric, and thermal technologies have their unique mechanisms. Each type of sensor is suitable for specific applications based on their characteristics.</p></p>
