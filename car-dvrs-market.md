<p><p>Car DVRs are available in two main market types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. Single Channel DVRs record footage from one camera, typically facing forward, providing a basic level of surveillance. On the other hand, Dual Channel DVRs have two cameras that capture footage from both the front and rear of the vehicle simultaneously, offering a more comprehensive view of the surrounding environment. Both types of DVRs are popular choices for drivers looking to improve safety and security on the road.</p></p>
