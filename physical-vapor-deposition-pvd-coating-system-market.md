<p><ul><li>Sputtering Deposition</li><li>Evaporation Deposition</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Coating System market is categorized into three types - Sputtering Deposition, Evaporation Deposition, and Others. Sputtering Deposition involves the bombardment of a target material with high-energy particles, causing atoms to be ejected and deposited onto a substrate. Evaporation Deposition utilizes heat to vaporize a material which then condenses onto a substrate. Other methods in PVD Coating System include cathodic arc deposition, ion plating, and pulsed laser deposition. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
