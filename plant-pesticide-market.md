<p><p>Plant pesticide market types include phytotoxin, phytogenous insect hormone, and others. Phytotoxin pesticides are substances that are toxic to plants, used to control weeds and other unwanted vegetation. Phytogenous insect hormone pesticides mimic the natural hormones of insects, disrupting their growth and development. Other types of pesticides may include biological agents, such as beneficial insects or microorganisms, and chemicals that act as repellents or attractants. These different types of pesticides offer a variety of methods for effectively controlling pests and protecting plants.</p></p>
