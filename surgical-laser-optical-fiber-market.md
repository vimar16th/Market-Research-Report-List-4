<p><p>The Surgical Laser Optical Fiber Market consists of two main types: Disposable and Reusable optical fibers. Disposable optical fibers are designed for single use and are discarded after one procedure, ensuring sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Reusable optical fibers, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used multiple times, making them more cost-effective in the long run. Both types play a crucial role in minimally invasive surgical procedures, providing precise delivery of laser energy for various medical applications.</p></p>
