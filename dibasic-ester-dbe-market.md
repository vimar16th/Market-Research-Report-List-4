<p><p>Dibasic Ester (DBE) is a versatile solvent that can be found in four main types: Dimethyl Adipate (DMA), Dimethyl Glutarate (DMG), Dimethyl Succinate (DMS), and mixed DBEs. DMA is commonly used in coatings and inks, DMG is known for its solvency power in metal cleaners and pesticides, and DMS is often found in adhesives and personal care products. Mixed DBE provides a combination of all these properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
