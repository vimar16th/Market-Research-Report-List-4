<p><p>Electric power transmission equipment market is divided into three main types: transformers, switchgear, and others. The "Others" category includes various equipment like insulators, transmission towers, and power cables. These different types of equipment play a crucial role in the efficient transmission of electrical power across the grid, ensuring reliable supply to consumers.</p></p>
