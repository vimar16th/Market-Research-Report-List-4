Frame cabinets provide a structural framework for electrical equipment. Each type caters to specific needs in the distribution of low-voltage power systems. Stationary cabinets are ideal for permanent installations, drawer cabinets for ease of access, and frame cabinets provide a solid structure for equipment.</p></p>
