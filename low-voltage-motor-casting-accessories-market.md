<p><p>The low voltage motor casting accessories market can be divided into two main types: frame and bracket. Frames provide structural support and protection for the motor components, while brackets are used to mount the motor securely in place. Frames are typically larger and more complex structures, designed to encase and shield the motor, while brackets are smaller and simpler, mainly serving to position the motor in the desired location. Both frame and bracket are essential components in ensuring the proper functioning and longevity of low voltage motors.</p></p>
