Part Toes prostheses are crafted to cover specific areas of the toe that may be missing or damaged, such as the tip or a section of the toe. Both types serve to restore the appearance of the toe, improve balance and gait, and enhance overall confidence and quality of life for individuals with missing or damaged toes.</p></p>
