<p><p>Phosphatic fertilizers are classified into different types based on their composition and nutrient content. Diammonium phosphate and monoammonium phosphate are both water-soluble fertilizers containing high levels of nitrogen and phosphorus. Superphosphate is a solid fertilizer that is readily available to plants and contains a high concentration of phosphorus. Other types of phosphatic fertilizers include triple superphosphate and ammonium phosphate. These fertilizers play a crucial role in providing essential nutrients for plant growth and are widely used in agriculture worldwide.</p></p>
