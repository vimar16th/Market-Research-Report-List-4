<p><p>A Static Cone Penetrometer is a device used to measure the cone resistance or the penetration resistance of soil. It is widely used in geotechnical engineering for determining the soil's strength and compaction properties. The device consists of a cone-shaped tip attached to a shaft, which is driven into the ground at a constant rate to measure the soil's resistance.</p><p>The Static Cone Penetrometer Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period, with a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14%.
