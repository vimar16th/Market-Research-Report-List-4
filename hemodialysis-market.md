<p><p>Hemodialysis is used in various medical conditions such as Acute Renal Failure, Chronic Renal Failure, Acute Drug Poisoning or Poison, and other conditions where the kidneys are unable to effectively filter waste from the blood. In Acute Renal Failure, hemodialysis is used as a temporary solution to support kidney function until they recover. In Chronic Renal Failure, it is a long-term treatment to replace lost kidney function. In cases of Acute Drug Poisoning or Poison, hemodialysis helps to remove toxins from the bloodstream.</p></p>
