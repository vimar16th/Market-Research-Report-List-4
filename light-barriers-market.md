<p><p>Light barriers are used in a variety of applications and come in different types based on their output configuration. PNP output light barriers are designed to switch a positive voltage when the barrier is interrupted, while NPN output light barriers switch a negative voltage. OSE output light barriers combine both PNP and NPN outputs in one sensor. Each type caters to different electronic systems and applications, providing flexibility and versatility in the market for light barriers.</p></p>
