<p><p>Automated welding systems can be categorized into multiple welding systems and robotic welding systems. Multiple welding systems involve machines that can perform multiple welding processes within a single system. On the other hand, robotic welding systems utilize programmable robots to carry out welding tasks with precision and efficiency. Both types of systems offer various benefits such as increased productivity, improved weld quality, and reduced labor costs. The market for robotic welding systems is expanding rapidly due to advancements in technology and increasing demand for automated manufacturing processes.</p></p>
