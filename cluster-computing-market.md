<p><p>Cluster computing can be categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On the other hand, cloud-based cluster computing involves utilizing cluster resources provided by a third-party cloud service provider, offering scalability and flexibility without the need for on-site infrastructure maintenance.
