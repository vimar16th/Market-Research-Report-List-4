<p><ul><li>Single Stage Liquid Ring Compressors</li><li>Two Stage Liquid Ring Compressors</li><li>Multistage Liquid Ring Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid ring compressors are classified into three types based on the number of stages: Single stage, Two stage, and Multistage. Single stage liquid ring compressors have one compression stage and are suitable for low to medium pressure applications. Two stage liquid ring compressors have two compression stages, allowing for higher pressures to be achieved. Multistage liquid ring compressors have three or more stages, offering the highest level of compression for demanding industrial processes. Each type of compressor is designed to meet specific pressure requirements in various applications.</p></p>
