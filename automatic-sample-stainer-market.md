<p><strong>The Automatic Sample Stainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Feed Stainer</li><li>Batch Stainer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic sample stainers are used in laboratory settings to stain tissue samples for analysis. There are two main types of automatic sample stainers - continuous feed stainers and batch stainers. Continuous feed stainers allow for a continuous flow of samples to be stained, making them ideal for high-throughput laboratories. On the other hand, batch stainers are designed to process a set number of samples in one batch, making them suitable for smaller laboratories or when specific staining protocols need to be followed for each batch.</p></p>
