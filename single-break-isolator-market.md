<p><strong>The Single Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single break isolators are commonly used in power systems to isolate equipment for maintenance or in case of a fault. They are classified based on their location and purpose in the system. Bus side isolators are installed on the busbars to isolate sections of the bus in case of maintenance. Line side isolators are installed on the feeder lines to isolate the incoming and outgoing power. Transfer bus side isolators are used in conjunction with a transfer bus to reroute power in case of a fault.</p></p>
