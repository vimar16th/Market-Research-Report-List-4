<p><p>Pharmaceutical excipients market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used. Cellulose and derivatives are commonly used for their binding and disintegrating properties. Starch and derivatives are widely used as fillers and diluents in pharmaceutical formulations. Inorganic salts are used for their buffering and solubility-enhancing properties. Medicinal sucrose is used as a sweetening agent and stabilizer in liquid formulations. Other excipients include polymers, alcohols, and amino acids, which serve various functions in pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
