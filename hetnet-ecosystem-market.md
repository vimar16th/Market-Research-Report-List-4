Picocells are larger than femtocells and are typically used in indoor environments such as offices or shopping malls. Microcells are larger still and are used to provide coverage in outdoor areas such as urban streets. These different market types cater to specific coverage and capacity needs within the overall HetNet ecosystem.</p></p>
