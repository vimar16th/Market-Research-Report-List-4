<p><strong>The Hemodialysis Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single pump hemodialysis machines are designed with a single pump system to perform dialysis treatment efficiently. These machines are cost-effective and simpler in design compared to double pump hemodialysis machines. On the other hand, double pump hemodialysis machines feature two separate pumps for blood and dialysate circulation, allowing for better control and customization of the dialysis process. While single pump machines are more commonly used in smaller healthcare facilities, double pump machines are preferred in larger settings for their advanced capabilities.</p></p>
