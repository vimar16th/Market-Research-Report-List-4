<p><strong>The Solid Shaft Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Solid Shaft Encoder</li><li>Absolute Solid Shaft Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Shaft Encoder Market consists of two main types: Incremental Solid Shaft Encoder and Absolute Solid Shaft Encoder. Incremental Solid Shaft Encoder provides relative position information by counting pulses generated by the encoder. On the other hand, Absolute Solid Shaft Encoder provides a unique digital code for each position, enabling it to maintain position information even during power loss. Both types are widely used in industrial applications such as robotics, motor control, and automated machinery for precise motion control and monitoring.</p></p>
