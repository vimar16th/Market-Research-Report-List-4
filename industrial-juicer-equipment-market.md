<p><p>Industrial juicer equipment market includes various types such as belt juicer, cup juicer, air bag juicer, spiral juicer, and others. Belt juicers utilize a rotating belt to extract juice from fruits and vegetables. Cup juicers operate by collecting juice in cups placed below the juicing mechanism. Air bag juicers use air pressure to squeeze juice from produce. Spiral juicers utilize a spiral auger to crush and extract juice. Other types of industrial juicers may include hydraulic juicers or masticating juicers, each with their unique juicing method.</p></p>
