<p><p>SOI (Silicon on Insulator) wafers are semiconductor wafers that consist of a thin layer of silicon on top of an insulating substrate, typically made of silicon dioxide. These wafers offer several advantages over traditional silicon wafers, including reduced power consumption, improved performance, and better radiation tolerance. SOI wafers are commonly used in applications such as smartphones, automotive electronics, and IoT devices.</p><p>The global SOI Wafer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.30% during the forecast period. The rise in the adoption of SOI technology in advanced computing, communication, and automotive applications is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the SOI wafer market is the growing adoption of FD-SOI (Fully Depleted SOI) technology, which offers even lower power consumption and higher performance compared to traditional SOI wafers.
