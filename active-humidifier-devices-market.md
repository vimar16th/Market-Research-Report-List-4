<p><p>Active humidifier devices market includes electronic humidifiers and bubble humidifiers. Electronic humidifiers are electrically powered devices that provide precise control over humidity levels in enclosed spaces by releasing water vapor. Bubble humidifiers, on the other hand, are non-electric devices commonly used in medical settings to humidify oxygen therapy. They consist of a container filled with water and a tube attached to the oxygen source, creating bubbles that humidify the oxygen before it is inhaled by the patient.</p></p>
