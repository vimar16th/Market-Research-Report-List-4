<p><p>A car immobilizer is an electronic security device that is installed in a vehicle to prevent the engine from starting unless the correct key or fob is present. This technology has become increasingly popular as a way to deter car theft and enhance vehicle security.</p><p>The car immobilizer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. <p><p>Car Immobilizer Market is divided into Type I and Type II based on their working mechanisms. On the other hand, Type II immobilizers use electronic systems that require a specific code or signal to start the engine, making them more secure. Both types aim to deter theft and increase vehicle security, but Type II immobilizers provide a higher level of protection due to their technology.</p></p>
