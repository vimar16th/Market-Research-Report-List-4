<p><p>Cell proliferation kits are used to measure the viability of cells in research and clinical settings. There are three main types of cell viability kits on the market: fluorometric, colorimetric, and bioluminescent. Fluorometric kits use fluorescent dyes to measure cell viability, while colorimetric kits use color changes to indicate viability. Bioluminescent kits use light emitted by cells to measure viability. Each type of kit offers different advantages and can be chosen based on the specific needs of the experiment or study.</p></p>
