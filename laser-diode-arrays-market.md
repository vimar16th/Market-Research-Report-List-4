<p><strong>The Laser Diode Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microchannel Cooled</li><li>Conductively Cooled</li><li>Fluid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser diode arrays are used in various applications and are categorized based on their cooling methods. Microchannel cooled arrays use a small channel to efficiently dissipate heat, making them ideal for high-power density applications. Conductively cooled arrays have a heat sink that absorbs and disperses heat through conduction, suitable for moderate power applications. Fluid cooled arrays use a liquid coolant to regulate temperature and are commonly used in high-power systems where precise heat management is crucial. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the application's requirements.</p></p>
