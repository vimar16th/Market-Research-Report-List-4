<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Polyvinyl Alcohol is available in two main types based on the degree of hydrolysis: Fully Hydrolized and Partially Hydrolized. Fully Hydrolized Polyvinyl Alcohol has undergone complete hydrolysis, resulting in a high degree of water solubility and film-forming properties. On the other hand, Partially Hydrolized Polyvinyl Alcohol has undergone partial hydrolysis, resulting in a lower degree of water solubility and film-forming properties compared to the fully hydrolized counterpart. Both types are widely used in various cosmetic applications.</p></p>
