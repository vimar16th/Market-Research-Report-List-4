<p><p>Milk and Dairy Analyzer markets are categorized as Ultrasonic and Infrared types. Ultrasonic analyzers use sound waves to measure the composition of milk, while Infrared analyzers use infrared light to detect molecule bonds. Both types provide accurate and efficient analysis of milk quality, fat content, protein levels, and other components. Ultrasonic technology is known for its precise measurements and fast results, while Infrared technology offers non-destructive analysis and is ideal for large-scale dairy operations. Both types are widely used in the dairy industry for quality control and production monitoring.</p></p>
