<p><p>Multistage centrifugal pumps are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal multistage centrifugal pumps are designed with multiple impellers stacked horizontally, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, vertical multistage centrifugal pumps have impellers stacked vertically, allowing them to be used in applications where high pressure is required. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
