<p><p>Non Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment market is categorized into various types such as X-Ray, Ultrasonic, Magnetic Particle, Penetrant Flaw Detection, Eddy Current Test, and Other markets. X-Ray equipment uses radiation to detect internal defects in materials. Ultrasonic testing uses sound waves to detect flaws. Magnetic particle testing identifies surface flaws using magnetic fields. Penetrant flaw detection uses dye to identify surface defects. Eddy current testing measures changes in electrical currents to find flaws. Other types include acoustic emission testing, thermal imaging, and visual inspection methods.</p></p>
