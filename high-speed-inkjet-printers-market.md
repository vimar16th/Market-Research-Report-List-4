<p><strong>The High Speed Inkjet Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Printers</li><li>Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed Inkjet Printers market includes two main types: Continuous Inkjet Printers and Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers. Continuous Inkjet Printers work by continuously propelling tiny droplets of ink onto the printing surface, allowing for high-speed printing of large volumes. Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers, on the other hand, only eject ink droplets when needed, resulting in precise and efficient printing. Both types of printers are widely used in various industries such as packaging, labeling, and commercial printing for their fast and high-quality printing capabilities.</p></p>
