<p><ul><li>Machine Milled Flour</li><li>Stone Ground Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic bread flour is available in two main types: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using modern machinery that efficiently grinds and processes the flour, resulting in a consistent and fine texture. On the other hand, stone ground flour is made by grinding the wheat kernels between two stone wheels, which retains more of the wheat germ and bran, giving the flour a nuttier flavor and coarser texture. Both types of flour are popular choices among consumers seeking high-quality organic bread flour.</p></p>
