<p><p>Air pollution monitors can be categorized into two main types: indoor air pollution monitors and outdoor air pollution monitors. Indoor air pollution monitors are designed to measure pollutants such as volatile organic compounds (VOCs), carbon monoxide, and particulate matter inside buildings. Outdoor air pollution monitors, on the other hand, are used to measure pollutants in the air outside, such as nitrogen dioxide, ozone, and sulfur dioxide. Both types of monitors play a crucial role in monitoring and improving air quality to protect human health and the environment.</p></p>
