Drilled settlement cells are installed by drilling a hole and inserting the device directly into the ground. French settlement cells involve placing the device in a pre-excavated hole and backfilling with concrete. Other market types may include pneumatic or hydraulic settlement cells, each with its own unique installation method and advantages for specific soil conditions. These devices provide accurate and reliable data for construction projects and geological studies.</p></p>
