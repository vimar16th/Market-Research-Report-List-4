<p><p>Meat speciation testing services are available through various methods including Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and other molecular diagnostic tests such as Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS). PCR is a widely used technique that amplifies and detects DNA in meat samples, while ELISA is an antibody-based test that identifies specific proteins. LC-MS/MS is a highly sensitive method that can detect and quantify multiple meat species in a single sample. These tests help ensure the accuracy and authenticity of meat products.</p></p>
