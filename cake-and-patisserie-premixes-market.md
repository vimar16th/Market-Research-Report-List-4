<p><ul><li>Complete mix</li><li>Dough-base mix</li><li>Dough concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cake and patisserie premixes are available in three main types - Complete mix, Dough-base mix, and Dough concentrates. Complete mix contains all the necessary ingredients in one package, making it convenient for bakers. Dough-base mix includes the essential dry ingredients, with the addition of water and fat required to produce the final product. Dough concentrates are high-quality mixes that require the addition of water, yeast, and sometimes fat to create the dough for various baked goods.</p></p>
