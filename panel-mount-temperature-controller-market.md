<p><p>Panel-mount temperature controllers are widely used in various industries for maintaining precise temperature control. There are three main types of panel-mount temperature controllers: switch controller, proportional controller, and PID controller. </p><p>Switch controllers are simple on/off devices, suitable for applications that do not require precise control. Proportional controllers provide continuous adjustment of the output based on the error signal. PID controllers use a combination of proportional, integral, and derivative control actions to achieve highly accurate and stable temperature control. Each type has its own strengths and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
