<p><p>Data Fiber Optic Cable Market types differ based on the type of optical fiber used - Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode cables have a smaller core size, allowing a single mode of light to pass through with minimal dispersion, making them ideal for long-distance communication. Multimode cables have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to pass through, making them suitable for shorter distances. Both types cater to different communication requirements and have their own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
