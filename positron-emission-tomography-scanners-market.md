<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanners can be classified into two main types based on the ring configuration: Full Ring PET Scanners and Partial Ring PET Scanners. Full Ring PET Scanners contain a complete ring of detectors around the patient, providing high-quality images with improved accuracy. On the other hand, Partial Ring PET Scanners have fewer detectors and are more cost-effective. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs and budget constraints of the healthcare facility.</p></p>
