<p><strong>The Effusion Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Effusion Cells</li><li>High Temperature Effusion Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Effusion cells are used in thin film deposition processes in various industries. Low temperature effusion cells are designed to operate at lower temperatures, typically up to 300°C, making them suitable for depositing organic materials. On the other hand, high temperature effusion cells can operate at temperatures exceeding 1000°C, allowing for the deposition of inorganic materials such as metals and oxides. Both types of effusion cells cater to different material requirements and are utilized in different applications within the thin film industry.</p></p>
