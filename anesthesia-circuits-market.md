<p><strong>The Anesthesia Circuits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-open Circuits</li><li>Open Circuits</li><li>Closed Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anesthesia circuits are devices used in anesthesia delivery systems to provide a pathway for the delivery of anesthesia gases to the patient and removal of exhaled gases. There are three main types of anesthesia circuits: semi-open circuits, open circuits, and closed circuits. Semi-open circuits allow for the patient to breathe fresh gas while partially rebreathing exhaled gases. Open circuits deliver only fresh gas to the patient and do not allow for rebreathing. Closed circuits recirculate exhaled gases after removing carbon dioxide and adding fresh gas, allowing for more efficient use of anesthetic gases.</p></p>
