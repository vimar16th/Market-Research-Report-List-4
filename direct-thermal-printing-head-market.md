<p><p>Direct Thermal Printing Head market is segmented into two types: Thick Film Printhead and Thin Film Printhead. Thick Film Printhead uses a thick layer of resistive material, allowing for high durability and long lifespan. On the other hand, Thin Film Printhead utilizes a thin layer of resistive material, providing high resolution and fine printing quality. Both types cater to different needs in the market, with Thick Film Printheads suitable for high-volume printing applications and Thin Film Printheads ideal for precise printing requirements.</p></p>
