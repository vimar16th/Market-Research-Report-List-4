<p><ul><li>Single Impulse</li><li>Double Impulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foot operated impulse sealers are commonly available in two types: single impulse and double impulse. Single impulse sealers use a single heating element to seal the packaging material, making them suitable for lighter applications. Double impulse sealers, on the other hand, use dual heating elements to create a stronger and more secure seal, making them ideal for heavier or thicker packaging materials. Both types of sealers are operated using a foot pedal, providing hands-free convenience for the user.</p></p>
