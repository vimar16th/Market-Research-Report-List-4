<p><p>A rotary sifter, also known as a rotary screen or rotary sieve, is a mechanical screening machine used to separate materials by size. Rotary sifters are commonly used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and chemicals.</p><p>The Rotary Sifter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.
