<p><p>Rehabilitation exercise equipment can be categorized into three main types: Lower Body Exercise Equipment, Upper Body Exercise Equipment, and Total Body Exercise Equipment. Lower body equipment focuses on strengthening and improving mobility in the legs, hips, and lower back. Upper body equipment targets the arms, shoulders, and upper back for enhanced strength and flexibility. Total body equipment is designed to provide a comprehensive workout for both upper and lower body muscle groups. These different types of equipment cater to individuals with varying rehabilitation needs, allowing for a well-rounded approach to fitness and recovery.</p></p>
