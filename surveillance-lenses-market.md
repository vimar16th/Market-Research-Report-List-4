<p><p>Surveillance lenses come in two main types: Auto Iris and Manual Iris. Auto Iris lenses adjust the iris automatically in response to changing lighting conditions, providing clear image quality at all times. On the other hand, Manual Iris lenses require manual adjustment of the iris setting to achieve the desired image quality. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, with Auto Iris offering convenience and adaptability, while Manual Iris allows for more precise control over the focus and exposure of the lens.</p></p>
