Manual examination couches are adjusted manually by the user, providing a cost-effective option for medical facilities. Hydraulic examination couches use hydraulic mechanisms to adjust the height and positioning of the couch, offering a smooth and efficient solution for patient examinations. Each type of examination couch caters to different preferences and needs in the medical industry.</p></p>
