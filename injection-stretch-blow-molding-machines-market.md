<p><strong>The Injection Stretch Blow Molding Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Type</li><li>Two-Stage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection stretch blow molding machines market types include single-stage type and two-stage type. In the single-stage type, the entire process of injection molding, stretching, and blowing is completed in a single machine in one continuous process, resulting in faster production. On the other hand, the two-stage type is a more common process where preforms are first injection molded in one machine and then transferred to another for stretching and blowing, allowing for increased precision and flexibility in producing complex shapes.</p></p>
