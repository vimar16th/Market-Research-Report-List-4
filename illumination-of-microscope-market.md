<p><p>Illumination of microscopes play a crucial role in enhancing visibility and clarity of the sample being observed. Two common types of illumination in the market are LED and Fluorescence. LED illumination is energy efficient and provides bright, white light for general observation. On the other hand, Fluorescence illumination uses specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescent dyes in the sample, allowing for detailed analysis of cellular structures and molecules. Both types of illumination cater to different needs in research, clinical, and educational settings.</p></p>
