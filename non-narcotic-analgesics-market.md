<p><p>Non-narcotic analgesics refer to medications that alleviate pain without causing addiction. The market includes two main types: Non-Steroid Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and Steroid Anti-inflammatory Drugs. NSAIDs like ibuprofen and aspirin reduce pain and inflammation by blocking the production of prostaglandins. Steroid anti-inflammatory drugs, on the other hand, like cortisone, work by suppressing the immune system and reducing inflammation in the body. Both types of drugs are widely used to manage pain and inflammation in conditions like arthritis, headaches, and injuries.</p></p>
