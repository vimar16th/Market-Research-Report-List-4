<p><ul><li>Cell-based</li><li>Bead-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow cytometry is a powerful technology used for analyzing cells in various fields such as research, diagnostics, and drug development. The market can be broadly classified into two types - cell-based and bead-based. Cell-based flow cytometry involves the analysis of individual cells in a fluid stream, providing information on cell size, complexity, and biomarker expression. On the other hand, bead-based flow cytometry utilizes microspheres coated with specific antibodies to capture and analyze target molecules or cells, offering high-throughput multiplexed assays. Both types of flow cytometry play essential roles in advancing biomedical research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
