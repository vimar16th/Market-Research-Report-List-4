<p><p>Pheromone monitors are used for pest control in agriculture. The four main types of pheromone monitors are bottle traps, funnel traps, delta traps, and others. Bottle traps are small containers with pheromones that attract pests. Funnel traps have a funnel entrance that leads pests into a container with pheromones. Delta traps have a triangular shape with a sticky surface inside to trap pests. Other types of pheromone monitors may include different designs or materials but serve the same purpose of attracting and trapping pests for monitoring and control.</p></p>
