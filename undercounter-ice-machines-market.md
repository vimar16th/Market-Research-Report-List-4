<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Ice Machines</li><li>Water-Cooled Ice Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Undercounter ice machines can be categorized into two main types: air-cooled ice machines and water-cooled ice machines. Air-cooled ice machines use air to cool the condenser coils, making them more energy-efficient and easier to install. On the other hand, water-cooled ice machines use water to cool the condenser coils, which can be more efficient in hot environments but may require more water usage. Both types are popular choices for commercial and residential use, offering different benefits depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
