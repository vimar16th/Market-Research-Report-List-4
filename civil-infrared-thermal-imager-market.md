<p><p>The Civil Infrared Thermal Imager market consists of two main types: Uncooled Infrared Thermal Imagers and Refrigeration Infrared Thermal Imagers. Uncooled Infrared Thermal Imagers do not require a cooling mechanism, making them more cost-effective and lightweight. They are commonly used for short-range applications. Refrigeration Infrared Thermal Imagers, on the other hand, are equipped with a cooling system, allowing for higher sensitivity and better image quality. They are typically used for long-range and high-precision applications. Both types play important roles in various civil applications such as surveillance, monitoring, and diagnostics.</p></p>
