<p><p>General Purpose Diamond Saw Blades are widely used in various industries such as construction, mining, and automotive for cutting hard materials like concrete, stone, and metal. There are three main types of diamond saw blades based on their manufacturing process – Sintering, High-Frequency Welding, and Laser Welding. </p><p>Sintering involves mixing diamond particles with metal powders and heat-pressing them into a circular shape. High-Frequency Welding uses high-frequency currents to bond the diamond segments to the blade. Laser Welding utilizes high-powered lasers to join the diamond segments to the core. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of durability, cutting speed, and precision.</p></p>
