<p><p>Vertical high-speed mixers are typically available in two main types - continuous type and batch type. Continuous type mixers operate continuously and are well-suited for high-volume production processes where ingredients are continuously added in one end of the mixer and the final product is discharged from the other end. On the other hand, batch type mixers are designed for smaller-scale production where a specific amount of ingredients are loaded into the mixing chamber, processed, and then unloaded before the next batch can be started.</p></p>
