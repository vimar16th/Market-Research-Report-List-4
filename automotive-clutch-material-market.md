<p><p>The automotive clutch material market includes various types of clutches such as friction clutch, centrifugal clutch, semi centrifugal clutch, hydraulic clutch, vacuum clutch, and electro-magnetic clutch. Friction clutches are most commonly used in manual vehicles, while centrifugal clutches are used in go-karts and lawnmowers. Semi centrifugal clutches are a combination of centrifugal and friction clutches. Hydraulic clutches use hydraulic fluid for operation, vacuum clutches use vacuum pressure, and electro-magnetic clutches use an electrical signal for engagement. Each type has its own specific applications and advantages in the automotive industry.</p></p>
