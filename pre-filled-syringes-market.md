<p><p>Pre-filled syringes are available in two main types: glass pre-filled syringes and plastic pre-filled syringes. Glass pre-filled syringes are made from high-quality borosilicate glass, providing better chemical resistance and compatibility with a wider range of drugs. Plastic pre-filled syringes, on the other hand, are typically made from polypropylene or cyclic olefin polymers, offering advantages in terms of break resistance, weight, and reduced cost. Both types of pre-filled syringes are widely used in the healthcare industry for convenient and safe administration of medications.</p></p>
