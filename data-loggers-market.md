<p><p>Data loggers are devices used to record and monitor environmental conditions over a period of time. There are three main types of data loggers: Mechanical data loggers, Electronic data loggers, and Wireless data loggers.</p><p>Mechanical data loggers use manual methods to record data, such as chart recorders. Electronic data loggers use sensors and microprocessors to store data digitally, offering more accurate and reliable results. Wireless data loggers transmit data wirelessly to a central server, allowing for real-time monitoring and analysis. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific monitoring needs.</p></p>
