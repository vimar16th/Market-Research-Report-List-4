<p><p>Intercoolers are heat exchangers used in turbocharged and supercharged engines to cool the compressed air before it enters the engine for combustion, increasing power and efficiency. There are two main types of intercoolers in the market: air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air intercoolers use ambient air to cool the compressed air, while air-to-water intercoolers use a liquid coolant to remove heat. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on factors like space constraints, efficiency, and performance requirements.</p></p>
