<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotated Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stress Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li><li>Strain Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotated Rheometer market includes stress controlled type and strain controlled type rheometers. Stress controlled type rotated rheometer applies a constant stress while measuring the resulting strain, providing data on material properties such as viscosity and elasticity. On the other hand, strain controlled type rotated rheometer applies a constant strain and measures the resulting stress, providing information on how a material responds to deformation. Both types are essential in studying the rheological behavior of various materials, including polymers, fluids, and soft solids.</p></p>
