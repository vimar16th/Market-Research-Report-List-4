<p><p>Human Body Composition Analyzers market includes various types such as Bioimpedance Analyzer, Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry, Skinfold Caliper, Air Displacement Plethysmography, Hydrostatic Weighing, and others. Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry uses x-rays to measure bone density and body composition. Skinfold Caliper measures body fat by pinching skinfold at various locations. Air Displacement Plethysmography measures body volume by air displacement. Hydrostatic Weighing measures body density by underwater weighing. Other methods include infrared interactance and near-infrared interactance.</p></p>
