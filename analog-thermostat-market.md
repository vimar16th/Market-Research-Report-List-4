<p><ul><li>Single Loop</li><li>Multiple Loops</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog thermostats on the market can be categorized into two main types: single loop and multiple loops. Single loop thermostats control one heating or cooling device in a system, such as a furnace or air conditioner. Multiple loop thermostats control multiple heating or cooling devices independently, allowing for more precise temperature control in different zones of a building. Both types of thermostats function through a simple mechanical or electromechanical system, making them reliable and cost-effective options for temperature regulation in various settings.</p></p>
