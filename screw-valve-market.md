<p><p>Screw valve market consists of various types including Screw Lift Valve and Screwed Globe Valves. Screw Lift Valve is designed to regulate the flow of liquids or gases by lifting or lowering a stem with a screw-like mechanism. On the other hand, Screwed Globe Valves are used to control flow within pipelines by opening or closing a hollow core with a screw-like stem. Both types of valves play a crucial role in the fluid control industry by offering precise flow regulation and reliable operation.</p></p>
