<p><p>Metal laser marking machines come in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention and are capable of running continuously without manual input. These machines are ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency is key. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human intervention, such as loading and unloading parts. This type of machine is suitable for smaller scale operations or for applications that require more flexibility and customization. Both types of machines offer precise and efficient marking capabilities for various metal materials.</p></p>
