<p><p>Marine emission sensors market consists of three main types - Extractive CEMs, In-Situ CEMs, and Portable CEMs. Extractive CEMs require samples to be collected and analyzed remotely from the monitoring location. In-Situ CEMs measure emissions directly at the source without the need for sample extraction. Portable CEMs are compact and easily transportable devices used for temporary emission monitoring. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, convenience, and cost-effectiveness for monitoring emissions in marine environments.</p></p>
