<p><p>Ethernet slip rings are used to transmit data signals in rotating equipment. They come in two main types: hollow-shaft slip rings and solid-shaft slip rings. </p><p>Hollow-shaft slip rings have a cavity in the center, allowing for cables to pass through and making them ideal for applications where space is limited. </p><p>Solid-shaft slip rings, on the other hand, have a solid core and are more robust and durable, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. Both types are essential for maintaining seamless communication in rotating machinery.</p></p>
